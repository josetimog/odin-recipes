Chicken Tinola Recipe

Description

Chicken Tinola is a Filipino soup dish. It involves cooking chicken pieces in ginger broth. It is a popular cold weather dish. I always feel warm and cozy every time I eat it. This Filipino Chicken Soup is best enjoyed with fish sauce as dipping sauce, and a cup of warm white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp minced ginger
  • 3 gloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup green papaya
  • 1 bunch spinach
  • 2 liters of water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Pour cooking oil in a pan and let it heat up for a minute.
  2. Add the garlic, onion and ginger and saute until fragrant
  3. Add chicken and let it cook until it is browned.
  4. Add the water, fish sauce and green papaya
  5. Cover and let the ingredients simmer for 20 to 30 minutes or until the chicken and green papaya are fully cooked.
  6. Uncover and add the spianch. Cover and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve with hot rice!
