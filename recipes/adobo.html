Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description

Filipino Chicken Adobo is the national dish of the Philippines and may well become your new favourite Asian chicken dish! Just a few everyday ingredients I can practically guarantee you already have, it is an effortless recipe that yields juicy, tender chicken coated in a sweet savoury glaze with little pops of heat from peppercorns.

This is a chicken thigh recipe and it MUST be made with thighs. No substituting with chicken breast!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • Half-cup soy sauce
  • Quarter-cup white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Black peppercorns
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 3 tbsp Cooking oil
  • Optional: 1 tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. Pour cooking oil in a pan and let it heat up for a minute.
  2. Add garlic and saute until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken thighs and let cook until the skin is slightly browned. The chicken does not have to be completely cooked.
  4. Add the soy sauce, black peppercorns, bay leaves, brown sugar and white vinegar.
  5. Cover and let the ingredients simmer for 20 to 30 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Serve with hot rice!
